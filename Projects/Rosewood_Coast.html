Rosewood Coast


We Are Rosewood Coast

Based out of Cincinnati, Rosewood Coast has, in the words of CincyMusic’s Courtney Phenicie, a “polished sound”, that should be “immediately added to your everyday playlists." The Queen City’s newest pop rock powerhouse formed in September 2017 and has since released their debut album on Spotify and Apple Music, with another single released on March 6th, 2020.
