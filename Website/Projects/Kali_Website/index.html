Kali Marsh

At First Glance

Kali Marsh is a singer, actress, and dancer currently residing in Cincinnati while studying musical theatre at Northern Kentucky University. She plans to pursue performing full time through theatre and as the lead singer of her pop-rock band, Rosewood Coast.

"JeShaun Jackson owns the stage in every ensemble role and Kali Marsh also controls the stage in her many roles."
