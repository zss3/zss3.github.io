About

Kali Marsh is a singer, actress, and dancer currently residing in Cincinnati while studying musical theatre at Northern Kentucky University.

In her young professional theatre career, Kali has portrayed a handful of diverse roles that demand a high level of singing and acting. She has been blessed to be a part of the Northern Kentucky University School of The Arts theatre company, holding roles in their production of Initiative in 2017 and Freaky Friday in 2019. Marsh is excited to use her talents in Cincinnati Music Theatre’s upcoming production of Bandstand.

As a pop singer, Kali and her band Rosewood Coast have been nominated for multiple Cincinnati Entertainment Awards. She has performed at events alongside major acts such as Grouplove, Yellowcard, and Drake Bell. Recently she traveled to Nashville to work with Grammy nominated and award winning producers on her band’s newest original song release, Feel Again.

Following the completion of her Musical Theatre degree, Marsh plans to pursue performing full time through theatre and as the lead singer of her pop-rock band, Rosewood Coast.
